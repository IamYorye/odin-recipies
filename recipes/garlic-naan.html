Garlic Naan

Naan is a leavened flatbread made from white flour. TYpically, yeast and yogurt are used to leaven the bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup water, room temperature
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 teaspoonn baking sodia
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup full-fat plain yogurt
  • 3/4 cup whole milk, room temperautre
  • Cooking oil, for the bowl
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 stick melted unstalted butter
  • Kosher salt

Steps

  1. In a glass measuring cup, combine the yeast, sugar, and water and let sit for 10 minutes
  2. In the meantime, mix the flour, baking soda, and baking powder in a large bowl
  3. Whish tokgether the yeast mixture, yogurt, and milk and stir into the dry ingredients
  4. Knead with your hands until the dough forms a smooth, soft ball, about2 minutes
  5. Place the dough in an iled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. LEave at room temperature until doubled in size, about 2 hours
  6. Remove the dough from the bowl and turn onto a well-floured surface. Knead it breiefly and divide into 12 equal portions
  7. ROll it into 1/4-inch thick ovals, approximately 6 inches wide
  8. Heat a cast-iron skillet over moderately high heat. SPrinkle each portion with minced garlic and press it into the dough
  9. Brush the dough with melted butter and place in skillet. Cook for about one minute, until the dough puffs up
  10. Flip, cover the pan, and cook for one minute. Remove from the pan and brush both sides with butter and sprinkle with salt.
  11. Place in a towel-lined bowl until ready to serve
