Neopolitan Pizza

Neopolitan Pizza, also known as Naples-style pizza, is a style of pizza made with tomatoes and mozzarella cheese.

The tomatoes must be either San Marzano tomatoes or Pomordorino del Vesuvio, which grow on the volcanic plains of the south of Mount Vesuvius.

Ingredients

  • 1 ball Best Homemade Pizza Dough
  • 1/3 cup Easy Pizza Sauce
  • 3 ounches fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Kosher salt
  • 2 basil leaves
  • Semolina flour or cornmeal, for dusting the pizza peel

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough using the best pizza dough recipe (follow this video instruction for more)
  2. Place a pizza stone in the oven and preheat to 500F. OR preheat your pizza oven
  3. Make the homemade pizza sauce
  4. Slice the mozzarella into 1/4 inch thick pieces. If it's incredibly watery fresh mozzarella, you may want to let it sit on a paper towl
  5. Remove moisture for about 15 minutes then dab the mozzarella with the paper towel to remove any additional moisuture
  6. When the oven is ready, stretch the dough into a circle; see how to stretch pizza dough for instructions
  7. Sprinkle a pizza peel with semonlina flour or cornmeal, then carefully place the dough on top
  8. Spread the pizza saucea cross the dough. Top with mozzarella cheese. Taste a bite of the mozzarella cheese
  9. If it does not taste salty, add a fiew pinches of Kosher salt to the pizza. If you're baking in an oven, add the basil leaves
  10. FOr baking in a standard oven, add the basil leaves after you bake (the leaves turn black in a standard oven)
  11. Transfer the pizza to the pizza stone on the pizza peel, then bake until the cheese is melted, about 7 minutes in the oven
  12. Slice into pieces and serve immediately
